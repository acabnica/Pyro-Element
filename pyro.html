Pyro Element

One of the most adaptable components is pyro.
Pyro users may utilize their elemental ability to merge with four other elements: electrical, cryo, hydro, and dendro.
Each reaction does extra damage to enemies, either in an AoE burst or by increasing damage over time.
All of the elemental reactions for pyro are useful since they can be utilized to easily take on any number of enemies.
